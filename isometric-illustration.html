When I was told about this project I had no idea on what to do. I searched endlessly for inspiration but the idea was not coming to me easily. There were so many complicated ideas that I had in mind but I had no idea how to fully execute them. It wasn't until I was standing at the bus stop and I thought to do the process of the 4 seasons.

I took the sketch I had and took it into illustrator. Then I started to work on each terrain for each season.

When the terrain was done for each season I decided to tackle the landscape first. I always like to start small because it allows me to figure out what style would look best for the illustration.
