About Me

If you don't know my name already it's Amber and I'm a Graphic Designer. When everyone asked me what I wanted to be when I grew up, I had no idea until I went to New York City. It wasn't until I was staring at a billboard that I said to myself "I want to do this". Having this experience has inspired me to try anything new and to jump feet first into any challenge.
