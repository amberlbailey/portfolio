This was the decided logo and colours with the final revision of rounding out the teeth more.

The target audience for this logo redesign are adults having or starting a family. They like to take the time to find the right dentist to trust and to keep coming back to. A dentist to recommend to others and to feel at ease when booking appointments for their family.

The goal is to visually show that there are many different dental services provided. That they are all provided in the oneÂ business. This goal was achieved by showing multiple teeth to represent that there is more than one dental practice.

After the revision was made I created a stationary package that was consistent throughout.

This logo is meant to represent trust, so new patients are at ease when making an appointment. Both colours used are cool and relaxing to represent the overall experience of going to an appointment.

This overall design effectively shows to patients that they can trust that there is everything to have them leave with a healthy smile.
